Restaurant Management

All-in-One Booking, POS, Kitchen Display System (KDS) & MRP Stock Automation

Core Features

🌐 Table Booking & Web Portal

Seamless customer table reservations via an online portal. Simple booking forms with mandatory fields validation, automatic email confirmation, and clean reservation workflows.

💳 Seamless POS Integration

Link reservations directly to Point of Sale restaurant tables. Load customer order history dynamically and ensure unified billing operations.

🍳 Real-Time Kitchen Display System (KDS)

State-of-the-art kitchen order queue tracking. Easily transition orders across To Prepare, Preparing, Prepared, and Served columns using a visual drag-free Kanban interface.

⚙️ Automated Stock Deduction (MRP)

Integrates with Odoo Manufacturing (MRP) to auto-create and confirm Manufacturing Orders (MOs) on order prep. Raw ingredients are consumed when prepared, and finished goods are deducted when served.

How the Flow Works

📝
1. Customer Bookings

Customers reserve tables online; reservation updates immediately in Odoo back-office.

🛒
2. POS Order Paid

Staff selects the reserved table on the POS screen, rings the dish, and processes payment.

🔥
3. Kitchen Prep

The chef views the ticket in the KDS and starts preparing, creating a backend Manufacturing Order.

🍽️
4. Serving & Stock

Food is marked prepared and served, triggering the ingredients consumption and finished food stock adjustments.

Technical Details

  • Fully Compatible with Odoo 19.0 Community Edition.
  • Dependencies: Base, Website, Point of Sale, Manufacturing (mrp), Accounting, Portal.
  • No additional modules or keys needed. Ready to install out of the box.
